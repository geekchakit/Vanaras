About Varanasi Tour

Varanasi also Known as Kashi,or Banaras is one of the world’s continually inhabited cities situated in the banks of holy river The Ganga.Darshan Rath Holidays Kashi Varanasi tour Package helps you to complete your Kashi Tour with utmost satisfaction....Kashi Vishwanath temple,also known as Golden temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is the 7th out of the 12 Jyotirlinga , main attraction here is the Kashi Vishwanath corridor which has transferred the Vishwanath Temple Yatra completely ,this infrastructural marvel has given a boost to tourism in the holy city. Residing Behind the Holy Ganga there are 80 Ghats out of which Assi Ghat,dashashwamedh ghat,Manikarnika Ghat are some of the popular Ghat, one cannot miss the Very famous Ganga Aarti held every evening Apart from temples, Varanasi offers its visitors a splendid tour of many historical establishments such as The Chunar Fort,Ramnagar fort,etc. One cannot afford to miss the street foods of Varanasi which is as elaborate as it’s history Our Varanasi tour package combines with nearby destinations of Sarnath, Ayodhya,Prayagraj and Gaya ji. Recent addition of Varanasi Tent city and The Ganga Villas Cruise is going to increase the flow of tourists who are planning banaras trip from around the world. Varanasi travel has a lot to offer to its upcoming visitors and we at Darshan Rath Holidays offers the best Varanasi tour package with affordable cost to fulfill your travel plans.
